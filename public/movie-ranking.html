The Best Movies According to KoalaPola

My top 3 movies of all-time.

1. Matilda

This is my childhood favorite movie of all time, fun and magical.

2. Harry Potter - all of them

All 90's kids know who Harry Potter is because we grew up together in their magical world.
Love how the character progresses througout the movie series, how we felt joy, experience pain, cried with them and feel their victories.

3. Mission Impossible

Love me some Tom Cruise movie.
I might say I like the most recent movies of the series, Dead Reckoning Part 1 is most thrilling and leaving just enough hanging to make you excited about what's next.
